This is the first post on `caisar.ai`. I'm planning to write about AI,
infrastructure, digital sovereignty, and the politics of AI policy — the
kinds of things I think about during the day and don't have a great place
to put them otherwise.

A few quick notes on what to expect:

- **Cadence:** irregular. I'd rather publish three good essays a year than
  twenty mediocre ones.
- **Format:** essays, not link-blog updates. If a thought fits in a tweet,
  it doesn't belong here.
- **Topics:** AI capabilities and deployment, infrastructure economics,
  EU digital sovereignty, and the politics around all of the above.

If you want to read more — or argue — you can find me by email
(`cesar@caisar.ai`).
